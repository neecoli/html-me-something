Background

I followed a nontraditional route to get here. I've always enjoyed the STEM fields. In high school, I aspired to be an astronaut. A lofty goal, and failing eyesight starting in 7th grade, I came back to earth and studied engineering and chemistry instead. With my thirst for knowledge and ambition, I then went into healthcare, taking my core problem solving skills to a new arena. During my journey, I served proudly in the military, alongside the nation's finest Sailors and Marines.



Along the way, I've realized that I still enjoy numbers, analyzing data, the technical aspects of problem solving. This led me back to my engineering roots, and into LaunchCode to learn the latest core programming and data science skills, which can be used in any industry to build things better.
